How to make a fruit salad.

Ingridients

- 1 apple - 1 orage - 1 pear - 1 kiwifruit - some grapes

Method

- wash all the fruit - cut the orage and squeeze the juice into a bowl - slice all the fruit then put the into the bowl - serve it with maple syrup, ice cream etc.
