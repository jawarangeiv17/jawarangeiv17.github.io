How to make a breakfast grill.

Ingridients

- 2 hash browns - 2 eggs - 2 rashers bacon - 2 breakfast sausages - 1 tomato - spring parsley - salt and pepper.

Method

- wash and pat dry the parslel and the tomatoes - place hash browns,sausages and tomatoes on a grill - cook the eggs in a pot of boiling water - serve on a warmed plate.
