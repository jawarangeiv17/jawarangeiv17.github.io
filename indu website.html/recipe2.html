How to cook a Chicken Caesar Salad.

Ingridients

- 120g of salad mix - 150g of shredded chicken - 3 bacon slices - 3 eggs - 30g parmesan cheese

dressing

- 60ml olive oil - 1t garlic - half a lemon - 5ml worcestershire sauce - salt and pepper.

Method

- mix all the dressing ingridients in one bowl -fry the bacon and boil the egg - add all of them together with the shredded chicken.
