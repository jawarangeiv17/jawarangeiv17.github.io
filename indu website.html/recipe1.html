How to cook a Waldorf Salad.

in this page, we will show you how to cook an woldorf salad.

Ingridients

- 125ml mayo or natura yoghurt - 200g celery - 2 crisp apples- 4 lettuce leaves - 60g of chopped walnuts - juice from half a lemon - salt and pepper

Method

- wash and chop the apples and the celery - toss them into the lemon juice and add the mayo or yoghurt and the walnuts - serve them on top of the lettuce.
